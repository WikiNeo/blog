Beyond the barricade, an old cavern corridor winds down into the earth, ending
in an ancient burial vault lit by a strange torch with a blue flame. Alcoves
line the walls, each one containing a rotten wooden coffin, while the center of
the room has a raised platform holding a stone sarcophagus.
