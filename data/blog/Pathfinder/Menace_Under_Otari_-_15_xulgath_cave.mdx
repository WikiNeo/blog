There is an overpowering stench in this chamber, like rotten fish mixed with
oily sweat. The warm, humid air only adds to the foul atmosphere. Unlike the
previous chambers, this one appears to be a natural cavern, dotted with
limestone stalactites and stalagmites, making it hard to see where the room
ends.
