This short hallway has smooth walls of worked stone with a door at either end,
though the door at the far end is closed. The floor is tiled with large, square
stone tiles.

## Chinese

这条短走廊的墙壁由打磨整齐的石块砌成，两端各有一扇门，但远端的门是关闭的。地面铺着一块块巨大的方形石砖。
